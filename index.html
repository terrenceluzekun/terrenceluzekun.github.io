ways for measuring business growth

Three ways of measuring business growth

Business growth is defined as “The process of improving some measure of an enterprise success” (https://cn.bing.com, 无日期) Before we measure the business growth we may firstly think about how to measure a business itself. In my opinion, number of people working in particular business is direct to indicate the size of a business because more labors could lead to larger production. Meanwhile, the market share of a business is also an indicator, larger percentage of market share means more power of controlling market.

In the beginning, I want to introduce some ways of measuring business growth before discussing the aims of measuring such growth. Firstly, the feedbacks from consumers could clearly reflect the change in some aspects in business such as quality of goods or service & prices of products from a business. Method for collecting consumer’s feedbacks includes questionnaire or other types of researches. If most of consumers think that the products are becoming better, it could be a kind of business growth. Secondly, the market share value may also be a good indicator while more shares in market or larger number of shareholders are the sign of expanding. More people purchasing shares in particular business means reputation of this business is better than before and the confidence of shareholders increase as well. I think these belong to business growth. Lastly, number of labors or capitals such as machines in an industry can indicate growth of business. Because labors and capitals are all factors of production, increase in these stuffs will lead to expansion of a business obviously, which is business growth.

After introducing three methods of measuring business growth, I think aims of measuring is conspicuous. Firms can adjust the ways of producing or managing through the measurement of growth, for example, if consumers are not satisfied with products of a firm after a period of time, change in quality of goods or prices of products are needed to be considered. Meanwhile, if little growth is achieved over a period, actions like hiring more labors or machines may need to take.

To sum up, I think frequent measurements of growth in business is necessary for formulate strategies. It is a very direct way to tell managers situation in a firm or industry and how to improve. Among the three methods above, I think collecting consumers’ feedbacks is the most useful because most of private businesses are profit maximized so production in business should be adjusted through consumers while they decide the amount of profit a business can make.

my feelings about medicine salon

In this article, I would like to share the experience and feelings of yesterday’s salon.

The main aim of this salon is to briefly introduce the whole industry of medicine which includes Traditional Chinese medicine, Western medicine (innovative medicine for curing cancer), biological medicine (vaccines) and medical instruments. Production chain of medicine is also mentioned. From the self-introduction of participants, I found that most of them are investors or doing jobs relating with stock market. This made me confused because it seems that Finance has no relation with Medicine. However, after I asked for some participants and searched on internet, I have some ideas about it.

Firstly, people can invest nearly every industry directly or in stock market. In fact, investment needs good estimating ability for trend of development of an area, which is directly related with profit and loss because time for purchasing and selling is important (in stock market). This needs deep cognition of the industry, or people cannot do correct judgement, this is obvious. So investors want to learn about different industries as more as possible.

But why medicine? Here is the second idea I come up with. Nowadays, novel coronavirus is spreading around the world, when there are lots of patients, in daily life, people still need to take protective measures like wearing masks. Large amounts of medical instruments, medicines, protective clothing are needed, which means demand for products of medicine industry is increasing sharply in this time period and this industry is developing quickly obviously. As a consequence, share-holders of companies of medicine earn large amount of money, making other investors interested about this industry in order to find potential opportunities of making profits. That’s why some investors come and learn medicine.

For instance, some investors are planning to invest for medicine companies which study and produce vaccines nowadays as novel coronavirus is very serious around the world while there is no vaccine for preventing this virus. As a result, many companies are trying to produce useful vaccines while many people are investing because once one company produce particular vaccine for coronavirus, this firm would make very large amount of profit. This may be more clear using number: a unit of vaccine can be expensive because the company produce it is monopoly, assuming one unit of vaccine is 300 RMB, may be billions of people are willing to purchase it for prevention. If two billion people purchase the vaccine, the money made is 60000000000 RMB, which may be much higher than researching and production cost and it will bring a hundred times profit to share-holders. As a consequence, more people are learning about medicine industry in this time period.

To sum up, I think this salon is very helpful to me, making me understand Economy is related with many other industries in the market. Also, I got to know something about medicine.

new ways for promotion on-line in China

​ As we all know, a whole new way for shopping has become very popular for 3 years since 2017 which is purchasing goods on-line on different platforms like “taobao” (china Alibaba). The process of buying is quite easy, firstly choosing goods you want and then you either put in shopping cart to think again before decision or buy them now. Next, pay for the good through “Alipay” (an APP for paying on-line), also, your house address and cell phone number are needed for courier to deliver your goods to you.

​ This is a graph for change in proportion of on-line purchasing revenue in total sale revenue. Indicating that on-line shopping is more welcomed in China.

​ Both pros and cons exist. Obviously, this way for purchasing is more convenient than traditional one because everything can be done at home. Also, prices of commodities are relatively cheap on-line while on-line shops don’t need to pay shop rents. Disadvantages like time lag and real quality, for instant, sizes of clothes may not be suitable for consumers sometimes. These are unknown when shopping on-line.

​ The main thing I want to mention is the brand new sales promotion. In the past, brands advertised their products through celebrity endorsement and putting publicity photos on the wall of street or inside shopping mall to arouse consumers’ interest. However, with short video APP (like Tik Tok) and live stream being more and more popular nowadays, different ways of promotion arise.

​ Firstly, short video platforms create some internet celebrities which have large number of fans on those platforms, thus they have commercial value. They can endorse for commodities of grands in short videos because many people pay attention on these celebrities and maybe more people would purchase goods after watching. Secondly, some people promote goods through live stream. These people promote commodities through using them lively and give some opinions after using, which make consumers feeling closer to product itself when others are using it. Usually, there is link of commodity on “Taobao” and consumers can click the link to purchase goods at any time.

​ This is a graph showing the trend of scale of live stream market and proportion of revenue. We can see that revenue made from live stream proportion is increasing.

​

​ In conclusion, improvement of on-line shopping revenue and different ways of promotion all represent progress of technology and changes of purchasing habits of consumers in China. For me, I prefer on-line shopping because of low prices, also, I don’t need to concern about quality of goods because I would be clear about it through live stream or short videos. However, unemployment should be concerned while decreasing number of people choose to buy commodities in shopping mall, when firms get less revenue, some labors will be fired.

Novel coronavirus pneumonia and economy

Up-to-date Economic phenomenon and my own feelings

Currently, the Novel coronavirus pneumonia (called disease in rest of the article) is spreading around the world. When the people around the world are taking action to guard against the disease, I find that the economic condition is changing significantly.

Firstly, some countries’ governments ask citizens not to go to work and students not to go to school in a period of time because of preventing further spreading of disease. In this case, industries can no longer produce goods and services, countries with tourism as main industry will not achieve tourists in few months and so on. Leading to aggregate supply and aggregate demand (like raw materials) decreasing. Also, in my opinion, the demand for products of people falls as well to basic goods like food and daily necessity in this special period. Upon factors can all lead to decrease in GDP (gross domestic production), which is a serious problem that may lead to recession around the world. These are just my ideas. Moreover, several evidences prove that my opinions may be right. Price of raw materials like oil decreases sharply in stock market, maybe because the demand for oil decreases.

In my expectation, some countries take actions to prevent the recession locally. I was very impressed of actions taken by America, which is reducing interest rate to 0. I think it is an incredible number because that nearly means preventing citizens from saving money but to borrowing money from banks. In my learning knowledge, reducing dealing with interest rate is monetary policy controlled by central bank. In this period of time, I think the action of America is obvious, which is using expansionary monetary policy to stimulate and resume economy locally because people will borrow my money, thus the purchasing power increases and consumption increases. Demand increasing may prevent economy from recession. This is interesting.

The facts above are from newspapers or observation on stock market while the impacts and reasons for imposing policies are all my own opinions. Lastly, I hope that the human can solve the problem of disease as soon as possible and everything will return to normal soon!

FBLA competition

Event: In this winter holiday, I took part in Future Business Leaders of America(FBLA). This competition is divided by two parts, team work and personal exam. There people can build up as a team. Our team’s topic is global business which include knowledge from micro part to macro part. For example, from mangement of factors of production in a company, which is micro, to the production of all kinds of goods and services in each firm and transaction in the whole market relating to demand and supply. Moreover, personal exam includes many different exams with different topics. I chose two topics which are insurance&risk management and business communication.
