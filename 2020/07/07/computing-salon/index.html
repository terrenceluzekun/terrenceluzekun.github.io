computing salon

​ Last week, I took part in a salon about computer industry. To be honest, I was a bit nervous because I am unfamiliar with computer. To my surprise, I still understood some points of the speech. Here is some information that I recorded, including my own understanding.

​ First part is Basic situation and characteristics of the industry, this industry is divided by hardware (The server), software (APPs) and service. New technology, which is cloud computing provides new applications like cryptocurrency and artificial intelligence. Second part is Basic framework of industry analysis, including typical business model of the industry and judgment on the core competitiveness of the company. Third part is things need to be avoided in this industry, while there are still some weaknesses in computing so money loss is a serious problem.

​ After the salon, I have several feelings, firstly, many industries nowadays need computing technology, indicating that on-line working is becoming more and more popular now. I can also find workings are becoming faster and more automated.
