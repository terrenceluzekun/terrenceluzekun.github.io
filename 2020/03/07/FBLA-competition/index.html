FBLA competition

Event: In this winter holiday, I took part in Future Business Leaders of America(FBLA). This competition is divided by two parts, team work and personal exam. There people can build up as a team. Our teamâ€™s topic is global business which include knowledge from micro part to macro part. For example, from mangement of factors of production in a company, which is micro, to the production of all kinds of goods and services in each firm and transaction in the whole market relating to demand and supply. Moreover, personal exam includes many different exams with different topics. I chose two topics which are insurance&risk management and business communication. The exam gave us some paragraphs relating to knowledge in exams, candidates should read the paragraphs and finish exams with their own knowledge accumulation as well.

Feelings: From team work, I deeply felt the impotance of cooperation. If all team members do the same thing together, there may be no enough time for us to finish the team work. Meaning that every member should choose several topics that they are good at to be finished in order to reach high efficiency. Also, trust among teammates is essential. From personal exams, I found that there is still large amount of economic konwledge waiting me to explore. In addition, the presentation ability and vocabulary need to be improved.

Move on and study hard !
