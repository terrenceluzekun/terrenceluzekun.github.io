Arduino

This summer vacation, I take part in an Electronic Engineering activity called “Arduino”. “Arduino is an open source computer hardware and software company, project and user community that designs and manufactures microcontroller-based kits for building digital devices and interactive objects that can sense and control the physical world.” (www.definitions.net/definition/Arduino, 无日期)

I want to introduce an interesting project I have done called Parking Distance Control (PDC). When something is close to the sensor, the buzzer would make higher frequency sound, when the object is far away from the sensor, the buzzer will make lower frequency sound or even no sound. In fact, this is a minified version of parking distance control on the cars.

Once my teacher introduced this project to us, he mentioned that the tools and sensors we use are much cheaper than the materials used to do a real parking sensor on car. This interested me a lot and I asked my teacher about the reasons for difference in prices. First, the quality of materials is different, a sensor on car can last for several years while the sensor using in this project could only last for several months. Second, the real sensor is much more precise, it has a swift response for change in distance because of its control system. Its measuring range is larger as well. As a consequence, accurate machines cost more money. This made me realize the relationship between Economics and engineering. Engineering can help people develop and produce goods which will be sold in the market so the relationship does exist. I have had a look at the stock market, there are companies of different industries and professions. In my opinion, as an investor, it is necessary to be familiar with relative industry. I want to be an investor in the future so I am delighted about the learning of Electronic Engineering.

These two are the pictures of Arduino, it is like a converter connecting the computer(codes) and electrical appliances.

The green one is distance sensor and the red one is buzzer.
